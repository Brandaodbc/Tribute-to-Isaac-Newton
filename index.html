Sir Isaac Newton

The first physicist.

The man who saw an apple fall from a tree, and was inspired to invent the theory of gravity.

If you have grappled with elementary physics then you know that he invented calculus and the three laws of motion upon which all of mechanics is based. More fundamentally, Newton's mathematical approach has become so basic to all of physics that he is generally regarded as the father of the clockwork universe: the first, and perhaps the greatest, physicist.
